Who Are We?

CoutureArangam is a luxury fashion brand dedicated to reviving and promoting the exquisite textiles of Tamil Nadu. Founded by a group of passionate artisans and fashion enthusiasts, our mission is to honor the age-old traditions of weaving while creating contemporary designs that resonate with today's discerning consumers.

The Textile Heritage of Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu boasts a textile history that dates back to the Sangam period (1st century BCE to 6th century CE), where weaving was a prominent craft. The region was renowned for its high-quality cotton and silk fabrics, which were highly sought after both locally and internationally.

Key Highlights of Tamil Nadu's Textile Legacy

  • Madras Checks: Originating from Madraspatnam, the famous Madras checks are a lightweight, handwoven cotton fabric characterized by vibrant colors and patterns.
  • Kanjeevaram Silks: Known for their opulence and durability, Kanjeevaram saris are crafted using a unique weaving technique called korvai.
  • Real Madras Handkerchief (RMHK): This handwoven checked fabric was once a staple in households and a popular export item.
  • Lungi: A traditional garment made from handwoven checked fabric, the lungi is a versatile piece worn by men across South Asia and Southeast Asia.

The Future of Tamil Nadu Textiles

At CoutureArangam, we recognize the challenges faced by traditional weavers in a rapidly changing market. Our commitment to preserving Tamil Nadu's textile heritage drives us to innovate while respecting the past.

Conclusion

CoutureArangam is more than just a fashion brand; it is a celebration of Tamil Nadu's rich textile heritage. Through our collections, we honor the craftsmanship, stories, and traditions that have shaped the region's identity.
